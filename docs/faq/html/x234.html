Where can I get GTK+? [GTK 2.x]

The canonical site is ftp://ftp.gtk.org/pub/gtk.

This site tends to get busy around the time of a new GTK+ release so try and use one of the mirror sites that are listed in ftp://ftp.gtk.org/etc/mirrors

Here's a few mirror sites to get you started:

  • Africa - ftp://ftp.is.co.za/applications/gimp
  • Australia - ftp://ftp.planetmirror.com/pub/gimp/gtk
  • Finland - ftp://ftp.funet.fi/pub/sci/graphics/packages/gimp/gtk
  • Germany - ftp://ftp.gwdg.de/pub/misc/grafik/gimp/gtk
  • Japan - ftp://sunsite.sut.ac.jp/pub/archives/packages/gimp/gtk
  • UK - http://www.mirror.ac.uk/sites/ftp.gimp.org/pub/gtk/
